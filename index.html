Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use for PrintForge?

We utilize a variety of engineering-grade materials depending on your project needs, including PLA+ for rapid iterations, PETG for durable mechanical parts, ABS for heat resistance, and TPU for flexible gaskets and dampeners.

Do you offer remote IT support?

Yes, for software optimization, virus removal, and network configuration, we offer secure remote support. Hardware diagnostics and repairs require the physical machine.

How long does a PrintForge order take to ship?

Standard stock items ship within 2-3 business days. Custom fabrication or large batch orders will have a lead time provided in your personalized quote.
